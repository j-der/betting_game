Betting Game

Welcome to a simple gambling game that uses Javascript, jQuery, and fake money!

Here's how it works:
You start off with $100 and will be asked to place a bet (between $5 and $10) on a number between 1 and 10. The computer generates a number at random and if you get the number exactly, you double your bet! If you're only off by 1, you keep your bet. Otherwise, you lose the bet. As in real life, the game is over when your money runs out.
